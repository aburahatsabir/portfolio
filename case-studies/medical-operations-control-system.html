The Challenge

Uncontrolled Cross-Border Operations

In high-volume medical tourism, a patient's journey involves critical dependencies. Handling this via ad-hoc emails and disconnected spreadsheets created a "Black Box" operational environment.

  • Revenue Leakage Risk Missed commissions due to lack of reconciliation between "MRP" and "Agent Rates."
  • Dependency Failures Patients arriving without valid Visa Invitation Letters (VIL), halting the sales cycle.
  • Data Fragmentation 49+ Hospital partners and 98+ Agents managed in silos, making performance reporting impossible.

The Solution

Visa-Gated Operations Ledger Architecture

I moved the organization to a relational operational architecture. By separating Data Definition (DD) from Transaction Logs, I created a system that enforces standardized data entry, ensuring every patient record is traceable, billable, and compliant.

To prevent "Data Drift", the Master Sheet validates against Data Definition files. No free text allowed for critical fields.
